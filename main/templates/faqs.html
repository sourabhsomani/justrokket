Frequently Asked Questions - Just Rokket

What is Just Rokket?

Just Rokket is an online platform that offers scholarships to study different courses in various top colleges and universities of India.

How can I get a scholarship on Just Rokket?

The process to acquire the scholarship is simple, fast and done entirely over the internet. You choose a scholarship available at a partner institution that fits your goals and budget, acquire the pre-registration and you are ready to enjoy the discount percentage until the end of your course.

What is the meaning of scholarship?

Scholarship implies the percentage of tuition fees waiver available to you from an institution. It is applicable on every instalment of tuition fees paid by you towards the course or qualification enrolled.

Do I need to pay something to get a scholarship?

In order to enjoy the scholarship, you are required to pay a pre-registration amount on our platform. Please refer to the pricing page on our website to get info on our current pricing policy.
Please note however that whatever pre registration amount you pay here is reimbursed by the college when you join it. Else, you have the option of refund ( as per our refund policy ).

In case you do not have a result for a competitive exam also, there will still be an offer for you from most educational institutions.
Applying early usually leads to higher scholarships.

Will the percentage of discount remain the same throughout?

Yes. Even if there is a revision in the college fees or if different fees is chargeable in different terms of the course, the percentage waiver of tuition fees available to you will remain the same.

Do I need to pay anything else after pre-registration?

No. After pre-registration, you are not required to pay anything else to Just Rokket and you will get the benefit of the scholarship for the full term of your course.

What is the Just Rokket scholarship voucher?

The Just Rokket scholarship voucher is the certificate of your payment of pre-registration amount with us and subsequent enrolment for the scholarship offer with the institution. It is to be submitted to the admitting institution as part of the admission documents.

Can I use the same pre-registration more than once on Just Rokket?

A selection of particular course and qualification and campus on a pre-registration voucher can be used in any another combination for the same college if the college agrees for the same. A voucher is non transferable to any academic year other than the one for which it has been issued.
Also pre-registration cannot be used in any college other than the one for which it is issued. In this case, you are required to make a new pre-registration, selecting the new institution and course. You can also contact us and request for the transfer of the pre-registration to another institution provided you have not used the Just Rokket scholarship voucher issued.

Is there anything more that needs to be done by me to avail the scholarship in the college ?

Actually nothing much! You only need to ensure that there is no discrepancy in the offer extended to you by the college as part of the admission offer provided to you through email or a printed copy, etc. In the rare case that there is one, the same needs to be brought to our notice asap so that we may work towards resolving the discrepancy.
Ensure that you are completing all steps required for selection confirmation by the institution, like for example any further assessments, submission of documents, fees, etc. As soon as the institution confirms your admission, the scholarship is also confirmed.

What is the procedure followed by the educational institution for confirming my admission?

The admission process followed by the concerned institution is the same applicable as when you would have applied directly. After the application, the institution will contact you and the necessary next steps will have to be finished including such steps like attending any further assessment steps, payment of fees (registration, etc.) and other admission process steps as are followed by the institution.

Can I get a scholarship if I am already enrolled in the institution?

Scholarships on Just Rokket are applicable for new students who do not have an admission offer from the institution for a given qualification/course and campus.

I have a scholarship for a course/qualification but now I want to transfer it to another course/qualification or campus within the same college.
Can I do it?

The scholarship on Just Rokket is applicable for the specific qualification and course (if chosen) and the campus. In case you want to change any of the specifications like qualification, course or campus, the rules and decision of the college will apply with regards to the transfer of scholarship to the new choice.

I paid my pre-registration amount but I did not join the institution. Will you return the amount paid?

Of course! We are committed to your satisfaction and will cancel your pre-registration and refund the pre-registration amount paid to us.
You should inform us of your request for cancellation of pre-registration and refund of the pre-registration amount, along with reason for the same, through your user account.
The maximum period to which you can apply for a refund of the pre-registration amount is till 15 days after the session of the institution, to which your were admitted, has started.
Please note that while we try to process a refund request within 15 days of its receipt, the refund process can be lengthy at times due to the fact that we have to notify the institution of your withdrawal and get confirmation from them that you did not enrol.
